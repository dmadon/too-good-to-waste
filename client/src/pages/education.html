Shopping Tips

  • Keep an inventory of items you throw away
  • Only buy stuff you like and you know you’ll eat
  • Create a reusable shopping list
  • Don’t do huge shopping trips
  • Buy produce the day you need them
  • Get fruits and veggies that last longer
  • Stock up on frozen foods
  • Know exactly what you want before you go shopping
  • Don’t shop when you’re hungry
  • Store and organize all your food properly when you get home
